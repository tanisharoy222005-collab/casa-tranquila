Rumtek Monastery

Distance: 2 km

One of Sikkim's most important Buddhist monasteries, known for its magnificent architecture, prayer halls, and peaceful Himalayan surroundings.

MG Marg

Distance: 12 km

Gangtok's vibrant pedestrian street filled with cafés, restaurants, souvenir shops, local handicrafts, and evening entertainment.

Tsomgo Lake

Distance: 40 km

A breathtaking glacial lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains. One of the most popular attractions in Sikkim.

Banjhakri Falls

Distance: 10 km

A beautiful waterfall and landscaped park showcasing local folklore, nature trails and scenic viewpoints.

Institute of Tibetology

Distance: 10 km

Discover rare Buddhist manuscripts, Tibetan artifacts, cultural exhibitions and centuries of Himalayan heritage.

Gangtok Ropeway

Distance: 12 km

Enjoy panoramic aerial views of Gangtok city, surrounding valleys and mountain landscapes.

Hanuman Tok

Distance: 18 km

A famous hilltop temple offering spectacular views of Gangtok and the Himalayan ranges.

Ganesh Tok

Distance: 15 km

A popular viewpoint with breathtaking views of Gangtok city and the majestic Kanchenjunga peaks.

Flower Exhibition Centre

Distance: 12 km

Home to vibrant orchids, seasonal flowers and exotic Himalayan plant species.

Zero Point

Distance: 150 km

Experience snow-covered Himalayan landscapes, dramatic mountain scenery and unforgettable views.
